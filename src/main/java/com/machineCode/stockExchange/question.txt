Functional Requirements
Place Orders:
Buyers and sellers can place orders (buy/sell) for specific stocks.
Order Matching:
Match buy and sell orders based on price and quantity.
Trade Execution:
Execute trades once orders are matched.
Order Book Management:
Maintain an order book for each stock.
Notifications:
Notify users of trade execution and order status.


Non-Functional Requirements
Scalability:
Handle a high volume of orders and trades.
Low Latency:
Match orders and execute trades with minimal delay.
Fault Tolerance:
Ensure system reliability and data integrity.
